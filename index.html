Bi-cycle Solution

Solve your Rubik's Cube using a simple two-cycle solution.

bi-cycle solution /ˈbīsikəl səˈlo͞oSHən/ (n) An artisanally crafted two-cycle Rubik's Cube solution consisting of four steps: edge permutation, corner permutation, edge orientation, and corner orientation. For a detail explanation, see the training companion.

Permutation

Move each piece to the correct location.

(UL UR)

(UBR UFL)

U F R U' R' U' R U R' F' R U R' U' R' F R F' U'

Orientation
